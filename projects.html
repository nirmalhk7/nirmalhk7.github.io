Crypto

(C++)


Crypto is a Windows Console application that works with Diffie Hellman principle. The individuals on the two ends can share their keys through a public medium and begin communication. Any eavesdropper who gets the keys cannot hack into the encryption.
Concepts Involved:OOPS, Diffie Hellman encryption.

First Aid Man

(Google Assistant)

First Aid Man was made with a fact in mind that most people acknowledge first aid knowledge is necessary, but nobody remembers first aid remembers clearly or likes to carry first aid information with them. Nobody keeps a First Aid app on their phones, and nobody's first instinct in case of a emergency would be to google the procedures. This is where First Aid Man comes in. It's a Google Assistant application, i.e. everybody has it on their phones. The application is oriented towards a commoner and not a doctor, i.e. it clearly states what to do and what to treat.. The procedures are taken from Band-Aid First Aid Guide, so they are accurate as well.
Concepts Involved:Dialogflow.
